The Story

After creating our award-winning plugin Fluid Pitch we wanted to stay true to our passion of elevating music expression in a way that’s never been imagined before! After months of iteration and development, we bring to you the V1 Beta of Fluid Chords.

A Word From Us

We aim to ensure that Fluid Chords is a completely stable plug-in.There are simply too many possibilities to test on our own because it supports so many workflows and use cases - hence we need your help! Your feedback not only helps us improve the current version of Fluid Chords but also influences the course of our development in the future. We sincerely thank you for participating in our BETA program.

- Team Pitch Innovations
